Meet the Clueless Coder

Chen (Jeff)

I've been teaching myself how to program for a few years now, spending a ton of time picking up books, trying out tools, and working through courses. The first programming language that made any sense to me was Python. I'm now learning the basics of the web, and hope to build sites using Flask and Python's data science stack in the future.

After all this time of working on other people's exercises on my own, I've come to two conclusions:

  • It's better to build, fail, and rebuild than follow someone else's exercises perfectly.
  • It's better to find people who are also struggling and work through your challenges together than it is to fumble around on your own.

Happy Coding, my fellow Clueless Coders!
